Healthcare & Maternal Health

Reducing mortality rates in Sierra Leone is at the core of our mission. We believe every mother and child deserves a fighting chance.

  • Provision of medical supplies and equipment to local clinics.
  • Support for nursing and midwifery staff training.
  • Emergency health funding for vulnerable families.

The Educational Legacy

Following in the footsteps of Pa Sweissy, we prioritize the training of the next generation. We don't just give fish; we teach how to fish.

  • Scholarship programs for gifted but underprivileged students.
  • Restoration of learning environments and school resource centers.
  • Providing laptops and digital tools for IPAM and university students.

UK Diaspora Community

As **Sweissy Union United Kingdom**, we look after our own. We act as a support network for Sierra Leoneans living in the UK.

  • Welfare support for members during times of hardship or bereavement.
  • Cultural integration programs for new Diaspora arrivals.
  • Annual gatherings to maintain the "Lightfoot Boston" heritage.
